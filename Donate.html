We Need Your Support!

We are committed to providing a safe, nurturing environment for those in need. At present, we have 14 residents, including 8 ladies and 6 gentlemen, who are being cared for at our facility. With nearly 30 new applications already received, the need for shelter and compassionate care continues to grow.

Your contribution towards the trust will benefit us in providing more facilities that would help the people of the ashram to lead a better lifestyle and health. It will also contribute in development of the premise and provide better facilities. We request you to contribute generously and be a part of this noble cause. Next Steps in Our Journey

We are currently planning to convert the existing kitchen and dining area into four rooms with attached bathrooms, allowing us to welcome eight additional residents into our care. Your generous contribution to the trust will help us expand our facilities, improve living conditions, and offer a healthier, more dignified lifestyle to those in need. It will also support the development of the premises and strengthen our ability to serve the community.

Developments Over the Past Year

The painting and renovation of the Vradhashram was done recently. It includes painting the building, relocating the Kitchen and developing the infrastructure of the premise. We are planning to expand the facility in order to accommodate 8 more people which would also need monetary support.

Previous Next


We had proposed building a new facility to relocate the existing hall and kitchen. Mr. Vasanth Prabhu from the USA graciously donated ₹5 lakhs towards the construction of a new kitchen and dining hall, with the total estimated cost being ₹8 lakhs. The newly built facility was inaugurated by Mr. Prabhu on 16th June 2025, marking a significant step toward improving amenities and fostering community care.
